== Short Description ==

Social Media Stats is a powerful social analytics tool that tracks and reports the performance of your posts and webpages at Facebook, Twitter, Google+, Pinterest, Linkedin and Stumbleupon from your WordPress Dashboard.

== Description ==

Social Media Stats is a social analytics plugin that tracks and reports the performance of your blog or website posts on social networks. People love to share great content with their friends on social networks, but do you know which network is the most popular for your blog/website or what type of blog posts are shared the most?

Social Media Stats helps you answer these questions so you focus on creating great content, the key to success for any blog or website.

With Social Media Stats you can:

* Track number of times a post is shared on Facebook, Twitter, Google+ , Linkedin, Pinterest and Stumbleupon
* Data is presented in a table view, easy to read and analyze 
* Heatmap to easily locate your most shared posts
* Total shares per social network
* Sort shares per social network and see at which social network your posts are shared the most.
* Sort shares per category or tag and see which type of posts are performing the best
* Sort shares per month
* Stats are updated on real time
* Cool widget to showcase your most shared posts

== Installation ==

1. Download zip file
2. Upload it using FTP or the WordPress installation feature
3. Activate it and you're done!

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Which social networks are supported? =

At the moment, Social Media Stats supports statistics on Twitter, Facebook, Google+, Linkedin, Pinterest and Stumbleupon

Simply go to https://wordpress.org/support/plugin/wpsocialstats

= What plugins for social media buttons does Social Media Stats support?

All social media buttons plugins are supported, however, feel free to contact our support team f you come across any issues.
